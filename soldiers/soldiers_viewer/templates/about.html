About

The data for this page is gathered from Burnley In The Great War, which is an excellent and useful site. I thought I'd make the data a little easier to browse and search through.

If you have questions about particular soldiers, or want to submit or correct information, you'd be best contacting the people who run Burnley In The Great War.
